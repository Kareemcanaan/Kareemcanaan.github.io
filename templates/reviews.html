Reviews

"The University of Manchester provides exceptional opportunities for students from all over the world. On the one hand, the Foundation Year programme significantly and deeply improves academic skills in mathematics and physics and, on the other hand, gives an opportunity to dive deep into university life, learn to think the different way and interact with your team as effectively as possible. The university enables to use language, cultural and country differences, enrich joint work, find an approach to solve common issues and achieve a common result. Up to this point, I have not had such an exciting experience when I could show not only a sense of perception, but also express individual thoughts and my point of view. It is absolutely impressive to know that I have new approaches to problem solving, time management, computer algorithms, methods of solving mathematical and physical problems. I look with hope to the future where I would be able to start my first year studies at the University of Manchester and begin an exciting journey of learning and new discoveries."
